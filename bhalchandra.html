Hacking

Hacking is the practice of exploiting vulnerabilities in systems or networks to gain unauthorized access or perform malicious actions. Hacking can be ethical or unethical, depending on the motive and the means of the hacker. Ethical hackers use their skills to help organizations improve their security, while unethical hackers use their skills to harm others.

Blockchain

Blockchain is a system of storing and transferring data using a distributed ledger that is maintained by a network of nodes. Blockchain data is encrypted, immutable, and transparent, making it resistant to tampering or fraud. Blockchain can be used for various applications such as cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, supply chain management, and more.
